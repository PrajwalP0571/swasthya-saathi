## 🎬 RECORDING TIPS

- **Read the script aloud 3 times before recording** — know it, don't read it
- **Slow down on results screen** — let judges actually read the scheme cards
- **Pause before the close** — 1 second of silence makes the final words land harder
- **Natural light on your face** — sit facing a window, not with light behind you
- **Don't re-record for small mistakes** — authenticity beats perfection for hackathons
- **Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or OneDrive** — paste the public link in submission
